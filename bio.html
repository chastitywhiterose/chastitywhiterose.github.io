Auto Biography of Chastity White Rose

My birth and legal name is Chandler Isaac Klebs. I have published books under that name and it's how most people know me. However I renamed myself Chastity White Rose in 2018 as a representation of my Vow of Chastity and because of my transgender identity.

I was born May 15 1987 in Merriam Kansas USA. My mother is Judena Klebs who besides raising me as a single mother and homeschooling me is also a music teacher and author of From Horror to Hope and From Rejection to Love. I was always very autistic and anti-social. I'm a genius in some ways but not good at human communication. I prefer computers and other species of animals. It's no surprise that I'm an ethical Vegan and that this has formed most of my views on religion, philosophy, and politics. To date I have only worked two different jobs in my adult life. The grocery store Hy-Vee and my current warehouse job in a federal facility that I dare not risk speaking about in detail but it's very physical! I do computer programming and digital art in what free time I get. I was also briefly in the Navy. That's why my mom still has the email address navyboymom@gmail.com

If there is more you would like to know about me and how I grew up let me know and I can add it to this page. Or you could probably just ask my mom!
